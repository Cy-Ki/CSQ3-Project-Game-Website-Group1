How to play

Press any key to go up (preferably not spacebar), release to go down. Avoid all the obstacles on the way by going through the gap between the obstacles, and avoid crashing into the borders of the games as it results in a game over.


Tips and Tricks:

If you have played the original Flappy Bird before, you know what to do. c:

As for those, try to be familiar with the appropriate timing to make the object continuously go up and down in a stable fashion.
